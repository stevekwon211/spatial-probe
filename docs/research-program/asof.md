# Stage 4 — ASOF (render-to-state acceptance): does converting a render representation (3DGS/NeRF) to a queryable 3D occupancy state preserve the action-relevant spatial signal a planner reads (free space, lateral clearance, free-path / corridor width), measurably and beyond what a volumetric overlap score (mIoU/RayIoU) reveals. HYBRID category (RIDE floor on Occ3D-nuScenes RayIoU under the public SparseOcc protocol + DEFINE a render-to-state property-preservation benchmark). Position #4 of 6 in the program order; reuses the occquery shared core (DDA raycast, OccupancyGrid, reachable_free_field, lateral_clearance / free_along_ego_path / min_free_width_along_path, the Occ3D adapter map_occupancy FREE/OCCUPIED/UNKNOWN, UnknownPolicy). Status today: PROVISIONAL, no code, no real numbers; the occquery core it depends on is real and tested (76 tests pass) but its own independent denotation oracle (occquery H3) does not yet exist. Argument structure is HYBRID-of-camps: a quasi-deductive non-identifiability backbone (a property an overlap metric provably cannot see) + an inductive ratio measurement on released fields.

## Motivation
"MOTIVATION + PRECISE GAP. The program thesis is '3D's essence is queryable/updatable STATE, not the render.' Render representations (3DGS/NeRF) are the dominant way the field now BUILDS 3D, and a growing line of work converts them to occupancy/ESDF to plan on (Splat-Nav, CATNIPS, GS-Occ-style conversions). The field evaluates that conversion almost exclusively with VOLUMETRIC OVERLAP (mIoU, and SparseOcc's RayIoU — itself a measurement reform that kills thick-surface inflation). The PRECISE GAP: overlap metrics score how much occupied/free volume agrees, but a PLANNER does not read volume — it reads free-space connectivity, lateral clearance, and corridor passability, which are non-local geometric properties an averaged overlap score can preserve in aggregate while corrupting locally (a single thin hallucinated floater off the centerline, or a sub-voxel-thinned wall, leaves RayIoU almost unchanged yet flips free_along_ego_path or min_free_width_along_path). NOBODY has a released metric that measures whether render→state preserves the ACTION-RELEVANT spatial signal specifically, nor a demonstration that overlap is insufficient for it. asof fills exactly that sliver: a render-to-state property-preservation benchmark, with (a) an oracle-free non-identifiability proof that overlap cannot see the property, (b) a run-both RayIoU floor so the field is not volumetrically garbage, and (c) a real-data decoupling showing overlap does not predict preservation. WHY NOW / WHY THIS LAB: the occquery shared core (DDA raycast, reachable_free_field, clearance/free-path/corridor predicates, the Occ3D adapter with its dense-GT-vs-masked distinction and UnknownPolicy) already IS the render→state property executor — asof reuses it unchanged, so the marginal cost is the render arm + the metric, not a new instrument. The honest current state: asof has no code and is PROVISIONAL; its RayIoU SOTA anchors and the GS-Occ3D code release are unconfirmed; on a Mac without CUDA it cannot FIT a render model and is therefore gated on a released render field/checkpoint — until one is consumable, asof ships the oracle-free H1 witness + the synthetic SMOKE harness and honestly reports the real arm as blocked-on-resource. This matters because if render→state does NOT preserve planner geometry, every downstream 'plan on a Gaussian splat' result inherits an unmeasured corruption — and asof's negative would be the finding that names it."

## Central hypothesis
CENTRAL HYPOTHESIS (one sentence, falsifiable by a single head-to-head): On the SAME scenes and the SAME downstream predicate executor, an occupancy state DERIVED from a render representation (3DGS/NeRF) preserves the planner-relevant spatial measurements — free space, lateral clearance, free-path passability, corridor width — that the occquery predicates read, to within a pre-registered band RELATIVE to the identical pipeline fed LiDAR/GT-derived occupancy; AND volumetric overlap (RayIoU/mIoU) is NOT a sufficient proxy for that preservation (a render→state field can match overlap yet destroy clearance/free-path denotation, and vice-versa). The load-bearing claim is the RELATIVE preservation ratio against a pre-registered run-both denominator, never an absolute RayIoU or absolute F1; the second clause makes the property-preservation metric ITSELF a falsifiable construct (the strongest move in the frontier corpus: reform measurement so a wrong claim is structurally hard to make). CRITICAL SCOPE INHERITANCE from occquery: asof tests whether the render→state conversion preserves the MEASUREMENT of static geometry; it does NOT assert any measured clearance/corridor is dangerous — danger requires relative motion over time and is the dynfield stage. asof's success/kill is preservation-of-measurement correctness, never danger-retrieval or navigation-success.

## Sub-hypotheses
- H1 — NON-IDENTIFIABILITY OF OVERLAP (oracle-free, the headline, quasi-deductive). There exist pairs of occupancy fields with (near-)identical volumetric overlap scores (RayIoU/mIoU within a tight epsilon against the same GT) that the occquery free-space/clearance predicates DISTINGUISH (different free_along_ego_path verdict, or |lateral_clearance| / min_free_width_along_path differing by more than one voxel / a pre-registered band). Constructed as a witness, exactly like occquery's expressivity witness: a render-style perturbation that an overlap metric averages away (e.g. a thin off-centerline hallucinated slab, or a sub-voxel-thinned wall) but that flips a passability/clearance read. This needs NO render model and NO oracle — it is a property of the metrics' definitions, checkable against the released RayIoU/SparseOcc implementation, and is preserved by construction across predicate rewrites. It establishes the gap that the rest of the paper measures: 'overlap is not preservation.'
- H2 — VOLUMETRIC FLOOR (third-party scored, RIDE). On Occ3D-nuScenes under the PUBLIC SparseOcc RayIoU protocol, a render-derived occupancy field (from a RELEASED 3DGS/NeRF→occupancy method, or a released converted field) attains RayIoU within a pre-registered fraction (target >= 0.90x) of the IDENTICAL pipeline fed the LiDAR/GT-derived occupancy. The denominator is pre-registered and BOTH arms are run (no cross-protocol borrowing). This is the externally-anchored floor that prevents the property metric from being graded on a field that is volumetrically garbage; it is reported as the public-protocol number, labeled as a reproduction unless the exact released checkpoint/split match is confirmed.
- H3 — PROPERTY PRESERVATION (the contribution, DEFINE; oracle = the LiDAR/GT-fed pipeline arm, RUN-BOTH not a self-graded constant). The render-derived field preserves the occquery predicate DENOTATION relative to the GT-fed arm: clearance-IoU / free-space connectivity-IoU / free-path-verdict agreement / corridor-width agreement are each within a pre-registered band, reported as full curves over the band, STABLE across the 3 UnknownPolicy rules (free/occupied/ignored), with per-scene bootstrap CIs. The 'oracle' here is not an external truth claim — it is the SAME deterministic predicate executor fed the GT-derived occupancy, so the comparison is render-field-vs-GT-field denotation agreement, not render-field-vs-absolute-truth (this dodges the circular-oracle trap: the GT arm is an independent SENSOR-derived field, not the render field re-read).
- H4 (decoupling sub-claim, makes the central second clause testable) — OVERLAP DOES NOT PREDICT PRESERVATION on real fields. The per-scene RayIoU and the per-scene property-preservation score are weakly/not monotonically related: ranking scenes by RayIoU does NOT rank them by clearance/free-path preservation (Spearman well below 1, with a pre-registered threshold and CI). This is the empirical counterpart of H1: it shows on real Occ3D data that a scene can pass the volumetric floor yet fail property preservation — the asof falsification surface and the reason the new metric earns its place.

## Success criteria (pre-registered)
- PRE-REGISTERED (declared before any real-field evaluation; written into experiments/asof_v0/PREREGISTRATION.md with frozen config + held-out scene IDs). FIXED EVALUATION BUDGET: pre-declare N scenes (>= the occquery val set), the exact Occ3D-nuScenes split, the exact released render→occupancy method/checkpoint (or released converted field) by commit/hash, the SparseOcc RayIoU protocol commit, and the predicate horizon/lookahead params — so 'preserves' is not retrofitted to whatever passed.
- SMOKE (NOT a scientific result, per the house rule): the property-preservation metric (clearance-IoU, free-space connectivity-IoU, free-path-verdict agreement, corridor-width MAE) COMPUTES on a TOY converted field and on a synthetic GT field where the answer is known by construction; the run-both ratio harness executes end-to-end (render-arm field, GT-arm field, ratio reported). Explicitly labeled synthetic-smoke / result_class='synthetic-smoke'.
- PUBLISHABLE (the bar for the paper, DEFINE+RIDE): (a) H2 floor — render-derived RayIoU >= 0.90x the IDENTICAL GT-fed pipeline on the pre-registered Occ3D-nuScenes split, BOTH arms run, denominator pre-registered; AND (b) H3 — clearance/free-path/corridor denotation preserved within the pre-registered band through the public occupancy-consuming predicate executor, reported as a FULL preservation-vs-band curve with per-scene bootstrap 95% CIs, STABLE across the 3 UnknownPolicy rules; AND (c) H1 witness holds as an executable test (identical-overlap pair, predicate distinguishes). The LOAD-BEARING claim is the RELATIVE preservation ratio and the relative RayIoU ratio; absolute RayIoU and absolute denotation F1 are secondary internal-validity checks only.
- STRONG (stretch, separately labeled): reproduce a released render-based closed-loop navigation result (Splat-Nav / CATNIPS) on a shared scene set as an additional anchor — explicitly flagged as RIDE-on-someone-else's-result, and EXPLICITLY NOT asof's primary contribution (asof's contribution is the property-preservation metric, not a nav win). Any nav number stays out of the headline because nav success is a danger/dynamics verdict (dynfield territory), not a static-preservation verdict.
- RELEASE criterion: the property-preservation metric ships as code + frozen config + the held-out scene IDs + the run-both denominator definition, so anyone can recompute it; otherwise it stays self-graded and is reported only as such (self-defined metric not released = not a result).

## Kill / pivot criteria (pre-registered)
- PRIMARY KILL (itself a publishable NEGATIVE, pre-registered as first-class): the render→state conversion PRESERVES mIoU/RayIoU (passes the H2 floor) but DESTROYS clearance/free-path/corridor denotation (H3 fails the band) — then 'render preserves state for planning' is FALSE, and that negative IS the result (it proves overlap metrics are blind to planner-relevant geometry, which is the field-level contribution). This is the asof falsification the docs already name.
- INVERSE KILL (collapses the new-metric justification, i.e. H1/H4 fail): if RayIoU and property-preservation are TIGHTLY monotone on real fields (H4 Spearman ~1) AND no identical-overlap/different-denotation witness can be constructed (H1 fails), then volumetric overlap already captures property preservation, the property-preservation metric adds nothing, and asof has no defensible contribution beyond re-citing RayIoU → pivot/abandon the DEFINE half, keep only the RIDE floor (not a paper on its own).
- UNKNOWN-POLICY KILL (inherited from occquery's kill shape): if the denotation-preservation verdict FLIPS across the 3 UnknownPolicy rules on the render-derived field (i.e. the result depends on the unknown policy rather than the geometry), shrink scope — restrict to scenes/fields where both arms are near-fully-observed (dense), report only there, and label the unobserved regime as out of scope (deferred to gt-distrust / vis-calibration / the predicted-occupancy robustness arm).
- DEPENDENCY KILL (gating): if occquery's independent denotation oracle (H3) is never built, asof's property-preservation H3 can still run as render-field-vs-GT-field AGREEMENT (no external truth needed), BUT if even the GT-fed arm cannot be trusted as the denominator (e.g. the GT Occ3D field itself is too sparse on the chosen scenes to give a stable predicate read), asof cannot establish a denominator → blocked until the occquery dense-GT premise is reconfirmed on the asof scene set.
- FEASIBILITY KILL (no-CUDA reality): if NO released render→occupancy field / checkpoint exists that can be consumed without fitting a 3DGS/NeRF locally (which needs a GPU asof does not have on a Mac), asof cannot produce the render arm at all on this hardware → the experiment is gated on either (i) a released converted occupancy field, (ii) a released render checkpoint + a CPU-feasible render→occupancy extraction, or (iii) GPU access; until one exists, asof stays at SMOKE on synthetic/GT fields and is honestly reported as not-yet-runnable.

## Oracle
"DENOMINATOR (not a truth oracle — a RUN-BOTH reference arm). The asof 'oracle' is the IDENTICAL deterministic predicate executor fed the LiDAR/GT-DERIVED Occ3D-nuScenes occupancy (via the existing adapters/occ3d.py map_occupancy with mask='none' = the dense accumulated GT, the same field occquery uses). Both the render-derived field and the GT-derived field are pushed through the SAME raycast/reachable_free_field/clearance/free-path code; preservation = agreement of the render arm vs the GT arm. This is deliberately a render-field-vs-sensor-field comparison, so it AVOIDS THE CIRCULAR-ORACLE TRAP: the reference is an INDEPENDENT sensor-derived field (LiDAR-accumulated Occ3D semantics), not the render field re-read through its own consistency, and not a human call on the same slice the predicate runs on (the exact circularity occquery H3's current 'visual-agreement' pass is flagged for). CONSTRUCTION + RELEASE: the GT arm is built by exact recomputation (the documented FREE/GROUND→FREE, all-else→OCCUPIED mapping at 0.4 m, ORIGIN/RANGE/GRID_SHAPE verified against labels.npz); release the conversion code + the held-out scene IDs + the run-both denominator definition so anyone recomputes both arms. CROSS-PROTOCOL GUARD: the H2 RayIoU floor uses the released SparseOcc protocol; do NOT compare that RayIoU to any number computed under a different split/modality/grid — match the protocol on both arms or label 'adjacent, not comparable'. INDEPENDENCE OF THE RENDER ARM: the render→occupancy field MUST come from a method that did not itself consume the GT Occ3D labels as supervision for the SAME scenes (else the render arm and the GT arm share a source and preservation is tautological); if the only available released method is GT-supervised, label that confound explicitly and treat the result as an upper bound on preservation, not evidence of it. VISIBILITY/MASK is protocol-defining and load-bearing: pre-register whether preservation is scored on dense (mask='none') fields only (recommended for v0, matches the occquery oracle premise) — the masked single-frame view is NOT the asof denominator and is deferred to gt-distrust/vis-calibration."

## Baselines
- BOX-ONLY / NAIVE BASELINE (the fairness control, inherited from occquery): the render→state value proposition is FREE-SPACE geometry (clearance/free-path/corridor) that object boxes cannot express — so the box-only baseline (distance_to_nearest_object over tracked boxes) is reported to show the SAME inexpressibility separation holds for the asof queries (a render-derived field, like the GT field, stores free space that boxes do not). This is the 'easy query the weaker representation should win' control that keeps the instrument honest.
- VOLUMETRIC-OVERLAP-ONLY BASELINE (the core baseline asof must beat to justify itself): RayIoU / mIoU as the INCUMBENT preservation proxy. asof's whole contribution is showing this baseline is INSUFFICIENT (H1 witness + H4 decoupling). If overlap already predicts property preservation, the property metric is redundant — so overlap-only is the baseline the new metric is measured against, not a competitor to beat on a shared score.
- GT-FED PIPELINE ARM (the run-both denominator, doubles as the reference baseline): the occquery predicates over the LiDAR/GT-derived Occ3D field — the ceiling the render arm is measured RELATIVE to. Pre-registered, both arms run.
- TRIVIAL / DEGENERATE CONVERSION CONTROLS (sanity baselines that SHOULD fail preservation, proving the metric has teeth): (a) an all-FREE field (passes nothing volumetrically, destroys clearance) and (b) an all-OCCUPIED field, and (c) a render field thresholded at a deliberately wrong opacity cutoff — each must register as preservation FAILURE; if a degenerate field scores as 'preserved', the metric is broken. Mirrors gt-distrust's shuffled-control / distance-baseline discipline.
- RESOLUTION-MATCHED CONTROL: a GT field re-voxelized to the render field's effective resolution (if it differs from 0.4 m), so the preservation gap is attributed to render→state CONVERSION error and not to a resolution mismatch — the stage-separation control.

## Ablations
- UNKNOWN-POLICY 3-RULE SENSITIVITY (the load-bearing geometric-mechanism ablation, inherited): run every preservation metric on the render arm under UnknownPolicy.FREE / OCCUPIED / IGNORED; denotation must be STABLE across all three or scope shrinks to dense-only. Maps to the unknown-policy kill. This is the one ablation that turns the unknown-voxel confound into a TEST.
- OVERLAP-vs-PROPERTY DECOUPLING (maps to H4): per-scene RayIoU vs per-scene property-preservation, correlation + CI — isolates the claim that overlap does not predict preservation. The single most important ablation for justifying the new metric.
- RESOLUTION-MATCHED ABLATION (stage-attribution): re-voxelize the GT field to the render field's effective resolution and recompute preservation — isolates render→state CONVERSION error from resolution mismatch. Report the null if resolution explains the gap (a cheaper-choice / honest-negative result).
- PREDICATE-EXECUTOR SELF-AGREEMENT (sanity, isolates executor error): GT-arm-vs-GT-arm preservation = 1 by construction; any deviation localizes a bug in the executor, not the conversion. One-factor isolation of the instrument itself.
- DEGENERATE-CONVERSION CONTROLS (metric-has-teeth): all-FREE, all-OCCUPIED, wrong-opacity-threshold render fields — each MUST register as preservation FAILURE; a NULL here (degenerate scores as preserved) is reported as the metric being broken, not buried. Mirrors gt-distrust's shuffled control.
- PROPERTY-COMPONENT ABLATION (which property is fragile): report clearance-IoU vs free-space-connectivity-IoU vs free-path-verdict vs corridor-width SEPARATELY, each mapped to one predicate — to expose WHICH planner property render→state corrupts first (expected: corridor-width / thin-structure passability is the most fragile, free-space connectivity the most robust). Report where a component is HARMED, not just where preserved (the occupancy-survey 'show where it hurts' rule).
- INTERACTION ABLATION (avoid the monotone-ladder trap): cross {UnknownPolicy × render-vs-GT source} rather than adding rows one at a time, to expose interaction between unknown handling and conversion error.
- OPACITY/THRESHOLD CURVE for render→occupancy (if the render arm is opacity-thresholded): preservation as a FULL curve over the threshold, pre-registered, so the result does not hinge on one movable cutoff.

## Metrics
"RELATIVE-VS-ABSOLUTE FRAMING (house rule, strictly enforced). LOAD-BEARING (relative): (1) RayIoU RATIO = render-arm RayIoU / GT-arm RayIoU under the SparseOcc protocol, target >= 0.90, pre-registered denominator, both arms run; (2) PROPERTY-PRESERVATION ratios/agreements vs the GT arm — clearance-IoU (IoU of the >threshold-clearance free region), free-space connectivity-IoU (IoU of the 8-connected reachable region from reachable_free_field), free-path-verdict agreement rate (free_along_ego_path render-vs-GT), corridor-width agreement (min_free_width_along_path render-vs-GT, |Δwidth| and tolerance-accuracy within a pre-registered band, NOT a single MAE), each reported as a FULL CURVE over its pre-registered band with per-scene bootstrap 95% CIs; (3) H4 decoupling = Spearman(RayIoU_per_scene, preservation_per_scene) with CI, pre-registered threshold. SECONDARY / INTERNAL-VALIDITY ONLY (absolute): absolute render-arm RayIoU, absolute denotation F1/agreement — reported for auditability, never the claim. EXPRESSIBILITY control: box-only inexpressibility coverage (free-space queries ~N vs box ~0), same as occquery. STABILITY: every preservation metric reported under all 3 UnknownPolicy rules; instability = the unknown-policy kill. NO single number is the headline — curves + CIs + the relative ratio are. Every self-chosen cutoff (0.90 ratio, the band widths, the Spearman threshold) is pre-registered and reported as a full curve a reviewer cannot move to flip the result."

## Expected results
"EXPECTED (if the central hypothesis holds): H1 witness constructs cleanly (an identical-overlap pair the predicate splits) — high confidence, it is a definitional construction like occquery's expressivity witness. H2 floor: a competent released render→occupancy field clears >= 0.90x GT-arm RayIoU on dense scenes — moderate confidence, contingent on a usable released field existing. H3: clearance/free-path/corridor denotation is preserved within band on MOST dense scenes but with a non-trivial tail of scenes where thin structures / hallucinated floaters from the render flip a passability or corridor-width read — this tail is the interesting result. H4: RayIoU and preservation are POSITIVELY but WEAKLY correlated (Spearman clearly below 1), i.e. overlap is necessary-not-sufficient for preservation — this is the expected headline that justifies the new metric. WHAT WOULD FALSIFY THE EXPECTATION (and what each implies): (a) if preservation is within band on ESSENTIALLY ALL scenes AND H4 Spearman ~1 → overlap already captures preservation, the new metric is redundant (inverse kill, abandon DEFINE half). (b) if the render arm passes RayIoU but preservation FAILS broadly (not just a tail) → the primary publishable-negative (overlap is blind to planner geometry; 'render preserves state' is false) — a valid, headline-worthy negative. (c) if the verdict FLIPS across the 3 UnknownPolicy rules → result depends on unknown handling not geometry → shrink to dense-only (unknown-policy kill). (d) if no degenerate control (all-FREE/all-OCCUPIED) registers as failure → the metric has no teeth → fix the metric before any claim. (e) if no released render field is CPU-consumable → asof cannot run the render arm on this hardware → stays at SMOKE, honestly reported as not-yet-runnable (feasibility kill). Per the synthetic-vs-real rule, the synthetic-smoke F1/agreement = 1.00-by-construction is NOT evidence; only the real released-field arm counts, and the real arm is EXPECTED to surface failure modes the synthetic arm hides (exactly as occquery's real-data audit collapsed synthetic F1=1.00 to real precision 0/5)."

## Scope — in
- IN: a render-to-state PROPERTY-PRESERVATION metric (clearance-IoU, free-space connectivity-IoU, free-path-verdict agreement, corridor-width agreement) defined over the existing occquery predicate executor, released as code + frozen config + held-out scene IDs.
- IN: the H1 non-identifiability witness (overlap-blind-to-property), as an executable test, oracle-free.
- IN: the H2 RIDE floor — render-derived RayIoU vs the IDENTICAL GT-fed pipeline under the public SparseOcc protocol, both arms run, denominator pre-registered (reproduction-labeled unless exact checkpoint/split match confirmed).
- IN: the H4 decoupling analysis — per-scene RayIoU vs per-scene property-preservation correlation, with CI, to show overlap does not predict preservation.
- IN: consuming RELEASED render representations / converted occupancy fields only (3DGS/NeRF checkpoints or released converted fields); the property extraction, the predicate executor, the ratio, and the metric are CPU/numpy and run locally on a Mac.
- IN: the 3-UnknownPolicy sensitivity report on the render arm, and per-scene bootstrap CIs, carried over from occquery/the cross-cutting rules.
- IN: dense (mask='none') fields as the v0 regime, matching the occquery dense-GT oracle premise.

## Scope — out (what it does NOT claim)
- OUT: any DANGER / safety / navigation-success claim. asof preserves the MEASUREMENT of static geometry; whether a preserved clearance/corridor is dangerous is relative-motion-over-time = the dynfield stage. A preserved 0.8 m gap beside a lead car is a correct preserved measurement, not a preserved hazard.
- OUT: FITTING / TRAINING a 3DGS or NeRF from scratch, or training any occupancy network — needs CUDA the Mac does not have; asof only CONSUMES released render fields/checkpoints. If none is consumable on CPU, asof stays at SMOKE and says so.
- OUT: asserting render→state is preserved in UNOBSERVED / occluded regions — the masked single-frame view is the conditioning variable for gt-distrust / vis-calibration, not the asof denominator; asof v0 scopes to dense fields.
- OUT: a closed-loop navigation result as the primary contribution (the Splat-Nav/CATNIPS reproduction is an explicitly-labeled STRONG stretch and a RIDE-on-others' result, never the headline).
- OUT: claiming the GT-derived Occ3D field is absolute ground truth — it is the run-both reference arm (a sensor-derived denominator), and any preservation result is render-vs-sensor-field AGREEMENT, not render-vs-reality.
- OUT: claiming render representations are GENERALLY better/worse than LiDAR occupancy — asof measures PRESERVATION THROUGH CONVERSION on a fixed pipeline, not which sensor/representation is superior.
- OUT: cross-protocol RayIoU comparison to published headline numbers (different split/modality/grid) — asof runs both arms under one protocol or labels 'adjacent, not comparable'.
- OUT: NL→predicate auto-compilation, a typed predicate algebra, any UI, and the other five experiments' logic — asof reuses the occquery predicates as-is.

## Threats to validity
- CIRCULAR ORACLE: if the released render→occupancy method was SUPERVISED on the same Occ3D GT it is compared against, the render arm and the GT arm share a source and preservation is partly tautological. Mitigation: prefer a render arm not trained on the target GT; if unavoidable, label it and treat preservation as an upper bound, not evidence. Also: the reference MUST be the sensor-derived GT field, never the render field re-read through its own rendering consistency (the trap occquery H3's 'visual-agreement' pass is already flagged for).
- STAGE-ATTRIBUTION (where does the error enter): a preservation gap could come from (i) render→state CONVERSION, (ii) a resolution mismatch between the render field and 0.4 m GT, or (iii) the predicate executor itself. Mitigation: the resolution-matched control isolates (ii); the GT-arm-vs-GT-arm self-agreement (= 1 by construction) isolates (iii); only the residual is attributed to (i) conversion.
- VISIBILITY/MASK as a silent difficulty knob: scoring on masked vs dense fields changes both RayIoU and the predicate reads; mixing them would make the ratio meaningless. Mitigation: pre-register dense (mask='none') as the v0 regime, exactly as occquery scopes its oracle; the masked regime is gt-distrust/vis-calibration's, not asof's.
- CROSS-PROTOCOL INCOMPARABILITY: RayIoU under the SparseOcc protocol is not comparable to any published RayIoU under a different split/modality/grid. Mitigation: run both arms under one frozen protocol; never borrow a published headline as the denominator.
- SELF-CHOSEN THRESHOLD: the 0.90 ratio, the preservation bands, the Spearman threshold are all movable. Mitigation: pre-register each and report the FULL curve; make the RELATIVE ratio/correlation the claim, not any cutoff.
- SYNTHETIC-TO-REAL OVERGENERALIZATION: synthetic-smoke success proves the harness, not preservation. Mitigation: house rule — synthetic is for hardening only; the real released-field arm is the sole evidence, and it is expected to expose render-specific failure modes (floaters, thin-surface thinning) invisible synthetically.
- GENERALIZATION-AXIS / SELECTION BIAS: the released render fields exist only for a handful of scenes; results on a cherry-picked easy subset over-claim. Mitigation: pre-declare the scene set and a seen-vs-unseen split before evaluation; report per-scene CIs and the failure tail, not a scene-averaged headline that hides decay (the occupancy-survey 'averaging hides the tail' warning).
- DEPENDENCY ON occquery: asof reuses occquery predicates whose own independent denotation oracle (H3) is not yet built. Mitigation: asof's H3 is render-vs-GT AGREEMENT (needs no external truth), so it is not blocked by occquery H3; but the occquery dense-GT-stability premise must be reconfirmed on the asof scene set, else the denominator is untrustworthy.
- NO-CUDA FEASIBILITY: the render arm may be impossible to produce on a Mac without a released field. Mitigation: state it as a hard gate; ship SMOKE + the H1 witness (both CPU/oracle-free) regardless, and report the real arm as blocked-on-resource rather than silently fabricating it.

## Argument chain
"INDUCTIVE/DEDUCTIVE CHAIN, evidence → conclusion. (1) DEDUCTIVE backbone (H1, oracle-free): from the DEFINITIONS of RayIoU/mIoU (volumetric/ray first-hit overlap) and of the occquery predicates (free-space connectivity, surface-to-surface gap), construct two fields with (near-)equal overlap that the predicates provably split → therefore overlap is NOT identifiable with property preservation. This is a construction/proof, not a score; like occquery's non-identifiability witness it cannot be sunk by an oracle dispute, so it is the headline. (2) INDUCTIVE measurement (H2/H3/H4) on released fields: push a released render-derived field and the sensor-derived GT field through the IDENTICAL predicate executor; measure (a) the volumetric floor ratio (H2), (b) the property-preservation agreement within pre-registered bands with CIs across 3 UnknownPolicy rules (H3), (c) the per-scene RayIoU-vs-preservation correlation (H4). The COUNTERFACTUAL that licenses the causal reading ('the gap is conversion error, not artefact'): the resolution-matched control + the GT-arm self-agreement remove the alternative explanations, so a residual preservation gap is attributed to render→state conversion. (3) CONCLUSION FORM (pick ONE lane, per the corpus): asof's PRIMARY lane is DIAGNOSIS — ship a property-preservation metric + a measured deficit, proven by the SIZE of the overlap-vs-preservation gap (H1 existence + H4 decoupling), with the named negative (preserve-RayIoU-destroy-denotation) as a first-class possible outcome.
