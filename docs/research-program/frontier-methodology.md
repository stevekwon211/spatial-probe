# Frontier spatial-intelligence paper methodology

Distilled from the 2026-06-21 frontier survey (occupancy / scene-retrieval / robotics free-space / benchmark methodology / paper structure / curation-uncertainty). How the field states hypotheses, pre-registers criteria, builds oracles, and avoids over-claiming. Read with [research-integrity.md](../research-integrity.md).

## 0. How to read this guide (de-duplication key)

These five surveys (occupancy/flow, AV scene retrieval + spatial-QA, robotics mapping/free-space geometry, 3D-perception evaluation methodology, embodied/VLM spatial reasoning, and uncertainty/occlusion/value) converge on ONE methodology with recurring failure modes. I collapse the 40+ surveyed papers into eight reusable rules. The single deepest cross-cutting finding: **the field advances its epistemics by reforming MEASUREMENT, not by posting accuracy. The strongest scientific moves in the entire corpus are new constructs — SparseOcc's RayIoU (ray-cast first-hit class+depth, kills thick-surface inflation), Waymo's flow-traced occupancy (occupancy must agree with flow BY CONSTRUCTION), PKL (planner-conditioned divergence), JIoU (IoU against a probabilistic label), the Waymo interactivity score (KL influence), and the 'train a vision-blind solver on the test set' shortcut detector.** Every one of those is a measurement device that makes a wrong claim structurally hard to make — which is exactly the band-aid-vs-root-cause distinction in your global rules: prefer making the wrong state unrepresentable over a per-symptom checklist. spatial-probe's expressivity witness (a non-identifiability construction, not a score) and its UnknownPolicy 3-rule sensitivity are already in this top tier. Naming convention used below: each section ends with **MAPS TO** giving the file:line evidence for where spatial-probe already embodies the rule (KEEP) and where it falls short (FIX).

**Maps to spatial-probe:** Synthesis spans all 5 surveys; spatial-probe grounding = PLAN.md + docs/benchmark-anchors.md (read in full) + docs/expressivity-vs-refav.md + docs/h3-real-data-findings.md + docs/m2-adapter-contract.md + CLAUDE.md + verified repo state (76 tests pass, Occ3D mini present, only occquery has code).

## 1. State ONE falsifiable central hypothesis + decomposed sub-hypotheses

FRONTIER PATTERN. The strongest papers state a single deficiency-attribution or mechanism claim engineered to be killed by ONE comparison, then decompose it into independently-testable sub-claims with DIFFERENT oracle dependence. SpatialVLM states it verbatim ('limited spatial reasoning is due to lack of 3D data') then intervenes and measures. Diffusion Policy decomposes one representational bet into three named desiderata (multimodality / high-dim / stable training), each a testable sub-claim. SparseOcc and Waymo Occupancy-Flow are exemplary because they make the METRIC itself a hypothesis (a second, orthogonal falsifiable claim). The anti-pattern (vague aim, 'X is better') is absent from the frontier and should stay absent.

THE RULE: (a) one sentence, one mechanism, falsifiable by a single head-to-head; (b) decompose so each sub-hypothesis names its own oracle dependence and its own kill condition; (c) the strongest decomposition gives at least ONE oracle-free sub-claim as the headline (an existence/expressivity claim that needs no ground truth), so the central result cannot be sunk by oracle disputes; (d) where possible, the non-identifiability form — construct two inputs the weaker representation MUST score identically, which the proposed one distinguishes (SparseOcc's gameable-mask argument, Waymo's flow-traced construction, and Why-Far-Looks-Up's counter-perspective decorrelation are all this shape).

A WARNING from the corpus: emergence/capability claims (RT-2) must be framed as EXISTENCE PROOFS plus an ablation that removes the cause — never as 'the model can do X' in general. Avoid universal claims a single counterexample kills.

**Maps to spatial-probe:** STRONG / KEEP: occquery is decomposed exactly right into H1 expressivity (oracle-free, headline), H2 retrieval competitiveness (third-party scored), H3 denotation correctness (released oracle) with each sub-claim's oracle dependence stated (PLAN.md:139-164; benchmark-anchors.md:112-113). H1 is the non-identifiability witness — two scenes with identical box+map observables, occupancy distinguishes (docs/expressivity-vs-refav.md:50-61; tests/test_expressivity.py, 3 tests pass) — this is best-in-class and matches SparseOcc/Waymo's 'make the construction the proof.' FIX: the other 5 experiments (dynfield/value-of-correction/asof/gt-distrust/vis-calibration) have one-line claims (PLAN.md:30-36) but NOT the H1/H2/H3 decomposition with per-sub-claim oracle dependence; benchmark-anchors.md gives them success tiers but not the explicit oracle-free-headline split occquery has. Before each is built, force the same decomposition: name its oracle-free sub-claim first.

## 2. Pre-register BOTH success AND kill/pivot criteria (the field's single biggest gap)

FRONTIER PATTERN — and the corpus's clearest collective weakness. **Across ~40 papers, formal pre-registration is essentially absent; success is defined post-hoc ('beat last year's SOTA') or externalized to a leaderboard.** The cleanest pre-registration in the entire set is borrowed from competitions (FB-OCC: CVPR'23 challenge fixes mIoU before submission; Waymo Occupancy-Flow). The substitutes the better papers use, which you should adopt deliberately: (a) a FIXED evaluation budget declared up front (RT-2 ~6k trials; OpenVLA 170+60 rollouts; Diffusion Policy 12 tasks × 4 benchmarks); (b) a PRE-STATED tolerance band for noisy continuous targets, chosen before reporting (SpatialVLM half-to-twice / 0.5×–2×; SpatialRGPT ±25%; VSI-Bench MRA averages over θ∈{0.5..0.95} so no single arbitrary band is over-trusted); (c) explicitly-enumerated KILL CONDITIONS as part of the criterion, not an afterthought.

KILL CONDITIONS are the rarest and most valuable artifact. Only a handful state a real one: SparseOcc (discarded empty voxels are unrecoverable — a cascading-error kill), OccWorld (cannot forecast newly-appearing agents), Why-Far-Looks-Up (lists THREE named falsifiers: if gains came from depth-supervision not data-scale / if proprietary models show ~0 gap / if cross-domain coherence is low), ActiveAD (if 30% does not match 100%, the 'most labels are low-value' claim fails). The universal informal kill condition recoverable from every necessity/value paper: 'if the added/removed quantity does not move the metric, the central claim fails' — but it is almost never written down.

THE RULE: for every experiment, write the success threshold AND the falsifying outcome AND the pivot, before running. Treat 'a publishable NEGATIVE is a valid outcome' as first-class (asof's kill — conversion preserves RayIoU but destroys clearance denotation — is explicitly framed as itself publishable). Report negatives as headlines, not footnotes (VSI-Bench reports CoT/SC/ToT making spatial accuracy WORSE; Schreier warns against trusting the very planner-centric metric class his lineage introduced).

**Maps to spatial-probe:** STRONG / KEEP — spatial-probe is AHEAD of the surveyed field here: it makes 'Negative/falsification conditions are part of the criterion, not an afterthought' an explicit cross-cutting rule (benchmark-anchors.md:37) and every one of the six topics carries a written Falsification clause (occquery PLAN.md:192-195 / anchors:107-110; dynfield:144-146; value-of-correction:170; asof:195-196; gt-distrust:221-222; vis-calibration:248-249). occquery's three-part success gate is explicit and conjunctive (PLAN.md:187-190). The dynfield kill is unusually sharp (dies if a field is necessary in EVERY regime OR sufficient NOWHERE — anchors:144-146), matching the Why-Far-Looks-Up multi-kill standard. FIX: (a) the success NUMBERS are still self-chosen thresholds (≥50 HOTA, ≥20 F1, ≥0.90 F1, RayIoU≥0.90×) — the docs acknowledge this is a vulnerability (anchors:28-31) and correctly make the RELATIVE gap load-bearing, but there is no FIXED EVALUATION BUDGET pre-declared (how many queries, how many scenes, how many seeds) the way RT-2/OpenVLA do — add a pre-registered N (PLAN targets '~20 queries' but only 4 exist: experiments/occquery_v0/queries.yaml). (b) The strongest move available: register the occquery success/kill as a /goal-style condition before M4 runs, since the criteria are provable from your own transcript (F1 deltas, HOTA, the 3-rule stability).

## 3. Oracle construction + the synthetic-vs-real distinction (manufacture-the-oracle, and never let it be circular)

FRONTIER PATTERN — the most structurally important and most under-flagged practice in the corpus. **The 'ground truth' is almost never hand-drawn; it is a MODEL OUTPUT or a CONSTRUCTION, so it inherits the construction's bias.** Two families:

(A) ACCUMULATE-THEN-RECONSTRUCT (all occupancy work): dense GT = aggregate multi-frame LiDAR (dynamic objects in box/object frame, static scene in global frame) + fill holes by surface reconstruction (Occ3D=VDBFusion/TSDF; SurroundOcc=Poisson; OpenOccupancy=pseudo-label augment + ~4000h human purify). The robotics distance-field camp does the deterministic version: build the oracle by EXACT geometric recomputation (Voxblox rasterizes a mesh and computes the exact EDT; FIESTA builds a K-D tree over final occupancy; nvblox computes voxel-to-surface distance). This is the field's reproducibility backbone — the oracle is re-derivable, no annotation ambiguity.

(B) MANUFACTURE-THE-ORACLE-VIA-GENERATION when the true quantity is unobservable (the uncertainty/occlusion survey is built on this): GLENet samples a CVAE 30× to define label variance; Labels-Are-Not-Perfect uses a generative LiDAR model + simulation as a real oracle sanity-check; CAPTURe uses a deterministic pattern-continuation rule so the hidden count is derivable; Beyond-the-Visible composites occluders so the occlusion-rate parameters ARE the labels. The honesty test (which separates rigorous from not): GLENet and Labels-Are-Not-Perfect ADMIT the oracle is a proxy; the SAM-distance paper leaves it implicit and is weaker for it.

THREE STRUCTURAL TRAPS the corpus names: (1) CIRCULAR ORACLE — the GT must not share the predicate's own data source / ray-cast (the synthetic-to-2D consistency or self-agreement is not external evidence). (2) VISIBILITY/OBSERVED-vs-OCCLUDED masking is load-bearing and protocol-defining — what counts as 'unobserved' silently sets the difficulty AND the metric, and is the single biggest source of cross-paper incomparability (Occ3D scores only camera-visible voxels; Waymo separates observed vs occluded; SparseOcc shows the visible-mask is gameable). (3) SYNTHETIC-FIRST is for HARDENING the instrument, not for producing a number — a controlled synthetic environment that decorrelates a confound (SpatialTunnel decouples vertical-position from depth; ProcFront decouples layout from instance) is a methodological contribution, but synthetic accuracy is never the result.

THE RULE: build the oracle by exact recomputation or explicit construction; RELEASE its construction code + held-out IDs so anyone can recompute; pick the truly-independent reference (different sensor/geometry, not the same field re-read); treat synthetic as confound-isolation + instrument-hardening, never as evidence.

**Maps to spatial-probe:** BEST-IN-CLASS / KEEP — spatial-probe's oracle discipline exceeds most surveyed papers. 'Self-oracle is never the headline; release its construction code + held-out IDs' is a written rule (anchors:25-27; PLAN.md:177) and oracle-dependence is a first-class ledger column (anchors:51-58). It explicitly avoids the circular oracle: gt-distrust drops the camera-mask-agreement leg because 'that shares the flag's own ray-cast, near-tautological' (anchors:216-218,223-225) and uses synthetic-occluder injection as a SELF-CONTROLLED oracle (anchors:211-213) — exactly the CAPTURe/Beyond-the-Visible manufacture-the-oracle pattern done honestly. The synthetic-vs-real rule is enforced three ways: 'Synthetic results are not scientific results' (PLAN.md:202-204; CLAUDE.md), three result classes kept distinct (anchors:17-21), and real data is valued for HARDENING not scoring — hand-labeling real Occ3D mini surfaced 3 predicate defects invisible on synthetic scenes, each fixed with a commit hash (docs/h3-real-data-findings.md:12-28). The visibility-mask is correctly treated as protocol-defining: the dense GT (~0 unknown) is the oracle, the per-frame mask (~88% unknown) is deliberately NOT applied to it and is instead the conditioning variable for gt-distrust/vis-calibration (PLAN.md:170-177; m2-adapter-contract.md:43-51). CRITICAL HONEST FLAG already self-caught: H3's current real-mini pass is only 'visual-agreement' because 'the GT is a human call on the SAME dense-GT slice the predicate runs on' — explicitly NOT external evidence, independent raw-LiDAR oracle deferred to v1 (h3-real-data-findings.md:6-10,87-89). FIX: H3 denotation P/R/F1 does not yet exist (only the visual-agreement pass); the independent oracle is the gating v1 deliverable — this is the single biggest distance between current state and a 'Publishable' tier.

## 4. Relative-vs-absolute metric claims + avoiding movable-threshold traps

FRONTIER PATTERN. Two opposed camps, and you must know which you are in. The INDUCTIVE/empirical camp (occupancy, mapping, VLA) headlines RELATIVE numbers on a shared oracle (FB-OCC 23.12→54.19 ladder; FIESTA 'order of magnitude over Voxblox'; nvblox '177×/31×'; Diffusion Policy 'average 46.9%'; OpenVLA '7× fewer params'), but pairs them with absolute per-task tables for auditability. The DEDUCTIVE/certified camp (IRIS, C-IRIS, HJ reachability, the calibration-framework paper) is ABSOLUTE/binary on the guarantee (free-or-not, safe-or-not, calibrated-or-not) and relative only on secondary size/runtime.

WHY RELATIVE IS LOAD-BEARING AND ABSOLUTE IS A TRAP: absolute mIoU/IoU are NOT comparable across papers because GT version, grid resolution, class set, and visibility mask differ (OpenOccupancy ~10-20 mIoU vs Occ3D ~28 measure different things). This is the 'no cross-protocol number comparison' rule: same metric name across different dataset version / split / input modality (camera-only vs multi-modal) / supervision / compute is NOT comparable — match all or label 'adjacent, not comparable.' The 3D-perception-evaluation survey's core anti-over-claiming move is to DISTRUST the absolute headline and convert it to a controlled comparison: normalized degradation (Robo3D mCE/mRR, Dong mCE deliberately decouple 'how robust' from 'how accurate when clean'), gap to a vision-blind solver, gap to a human oracle (VSI-Bench 33.8pt), a counterfactual accuracy gap Δ, a correlation-to-driving, or a directional A→B matrix vs a same-domain oracle.

THE MOVABLE-THRESHOLD TRAP: any self-chosen cutoff (0.90 F1, ±25%, 2×, 80%) is a vulnerability — a reviewer can move it and the result evaporates. The defenses: (a) pre-register the cutoff AND report the FULL CONTINUOUS CURVE (per-horizon decay curves are the corpus's soft falsification surface — OccWorld 1/2/3s, Cam4DOcc 0.5→2.0s, Waymo to 8s; averaging over horizons in the headline HIDES decay and is an over-claim); (b) make the RELATIVE gap the load-bearing claim, not the absolute number; (c) for ratio metrics, run BOTH arms and pre-register the denominator; (d) for continuous targets convert to a tolerance band that averages over thresholds (MRA) so no single band is over-trusted; (e) report variance/CIs — the surveyed inductive papers almost never report confidence intervals (point estimates + dataset averages only), which is a real gap a rigorous lab should close.

**Maps to spatial-probe:** BEST-IN-CLASS / KEEP — spatial-probe's metric discipline is more explicit than nearly every surveyed paper. The three rules are written verbatim: 'Self-chosen thresholds are a vulnerability… must be pre-registered AND reported as a full continuous curve… Where a relative claim exists (≥20 F1 over box-only, beat-random), that is the load-bearing claim' (anchors:28-31; PLAN.md:184-185); 'No cross-protocol number comparison' (anchors:32-34; applied at PLAN.md:161-163 flagging Occ3D mIoU as modality-dependent/PROVISIONAL); and 'absolute F1 ≥0.90 is a secondary internal-validity check' while the ≥20-F1 relative gap is load-bearing (PLAN.md:160; anchors:99-102) — this is exactly the Robo3D-style decouple. Pre-registered denominator + run-both is already specified for asof's ratio metric (anchors:191-193 'RayIoU ≥0.90× the IDENTICAL pipeline fed GT, pre-registered denominator, run both'). Variance bands / CIs are specified where they matter (dynfield seed-variance bands anchors:138-140; value-of-correction ≥3 seeds + full utility-vs-budget curve anchors:166-168; vis-calibration per-scene bootstrap 95% CIs + reliability diagrams + bin sensitivity 'not a single ECE' anchors:243-247). The do-NOT-cite list of stale/mislabeled cross-protocol numbers (anchors:60-64) is the verification analogue. FIX: occquery's own success numbers are still self-chosen and the '~20 queries → full curve' is aspirational (4 queries exist, queries.yaml); the 'full continuous curve' for the F1 gap and clearance MAE/tolerance is specified (PLAN.md:184) but not yet produced because there are no real numbers. Carry the seed/CI discipline (already present for stages 2/3/6) INTO occquery's eventual H3 report — it is not yet written there.

## 5. MECE ablation design (claim-mapped, not roadmap-laddered)

FRONTIER PATTERN — and a place where the occupancy survey reveals a weakness to AVOID. The dominant occupancy/VLA pattern is the CUMULATIVE ROADMAP LADDER (FB-OCC 23.12→…→54.19 adding one component per row; Cam4DOcc four baseline families static-world→end-to-end; OpenOccupancy's resolution/backbone/sweeps tables). This proves each addition helps GIVEN THE OTHERS but rarely isolates interactions or shows where a component HURTS — it is monotone-by-construction and weaker than it looks. The STRONGER pattern from the robotics + embodied + evaluation surveys is MECE / one-factor-at-a-time, each ablation mapped to exactly ONE sub-claim or design decision: Diffusion Policy isolates backbone (CNN vs Transformer) / action-horizon / inference-steps / action-space, each tied to a hypothesized desideratum; RT-2 runs a clean 2×2 (model-size × training-recipe) to separate 'is it scale or is it the recipe' (answer: both, but recipe is necessary); FIESTA sweeps neighbor-connectivity {6,18,26,24,32} and indexing/block-size to isolate the GEOMETRIC mechanism; Voxfield ablates projective-vs-non-projective TSDF (the one core idea); SpatialRGPT knocks out each of its two named innovations (RGB-only vs +depth-plugin; AABB vs OBB labels). The 3D-perception-eval camp adds the diagnostic ablation: CAPTURe feeds visible coordinates (25.66→17.34 sMAPE) to split visual-counting from occlusion-inference; the LLM/model-swap axis is now standard (RefAV swaps Claude/Gemini/Qwen and reports a PROGRAM-FAILURE rate alongside HOTA).

TWO HIGH-VALUE RULES the corpus teaches: (1) A NULL or NEGATIVE ablation is reported as evidence FOR a cheaper choice, not buried (OpenVLA 224 vs 384 'no difference' → use 224; ConceptGraphs LLM-vs-greedy association 'minuscule improvement' → keep greedy, reshaped the design). (2) Show where a component HURTS — the ladder never does this; the MECE design can (visible-mask training INFLATES foreground but DEGRADES background in SparseOcc, used as evidence the old protocol is gameable).

THE RULE: each ablation isolates ONE factor and maps back to ONE sub-claim/decision; report nulls and harms; for any synthesized-program or model-in-the-loop system, ablate the model/LLM axis WITH a failure-rate; isolate the GEOMETRIC mechanism specifically (not generic hyperparameters).

**Maps to spatial-probe:** PARTIAL / KEEP-AND-EXTEND. The single most important MECE ablation in spatial-probe is built and load-bearing: the UnknownPolicy 3-rule sensitivity (free/occupied/ignored), where denotation must be STABLE across all three or scope shrinks — this is the geometric-mechanism-isolation done right (PLAN.md:166-177,190; anchors:99-102,108-110; src/probe/grid.py UnknownPolicy enum, encoding UNKNOWN=-1). It is what makes the unknown-voxel confound a TEST rather than a nuisance (m2-adapter-contract.md:43-51 'the UNKNOWN mapping is what makes the 3-policy report real on actual sensor coverage'). The expressivity baseline is a clean fairness control (objects.py distance_to_nearest_object kept structurally separate as the box-only baseline — the 'easy query the weaker rep should win' from PLAN.md:18). The future stages already specify MECE axes (dynfield {field × regime} matrix with a mandatory no-dynamics control gate to catch harness leakage, anchors:136-140,147-148; value-of-correction value-ranked vs random vs geometric-without-prioritization, anchors:166-169; gt-distrust beats a shuffled control AND a distance-from-ego baseline, anchors:217-219; vis-calibration observed-vs-unobserved stratification, anchors:241-245). NEGATIVE-ablation-as-design-evidence is the actual development pattern: the v0→v1 C-space rewrite was driven by a NEGATIVE finding (0/5 precision audit) and the cluster-size mc=5 fix was DROPPED when it turned out wrong for a lead car (h3-real-data-findings.md:93-121,189-209) — that is ConceptGraphs-style honest negative-driven redesign. ## 6. Inductive vs deductive structure + the four logical fallacies to refuse

FRONTIER PATTERN. Argument type CORRELATES with subfield: perception/mapping/VLA argue INDUCTIVELY (measure error+timing, generalize to 'better representation', ship code — revisable by the next faster system); free-space/safety geometry argues DEDUCTIVELY (prove the property — separating-hyperplane / SOS / HJI PDE — then demonstrate scalability; permanent given assumptions). The BEST inductive papers add a near-deductive 'why it should work' scaffold derived from first principles or named failure modes of alternatives (Diffusion Policy derives 3 desiderata + cites EBM instability; SparseOcc reasons '>90% voxels empty ⇒ sparse suffices'; the calibration-framework paper and the Waymo KL-interactivity score DEFINE the target analytically then confirm empirically — these hybrids resist the SOTA-overfit critique best). The deductive camp's honest limit: a wrong dynamics/geometry MODEL degrades the guarantee but the algorithm 'does not fail a test' — soundness is theorem-level, scope is the only failure surface.

THE FOUR FALLACIES THIS CORPUS IS STRUCTURALLY PRONE TO, each with the corpus's own antidote:
(1) AFFIRMING-THE-CONSEQUENT — 'my method scored high ⇒ my mechanism is why.' Antidote = the remove-it/add-it counterfactual (RT-2 ablates away web-pretraining and emergence vanishes; the Waymo paper removes the high-interactivity agent and the AV trajectory moves MORE than removing the nearest agent; CAPTURe supplies coordinates). High score is necessary, never sufficient, for the mechanism claim.
(2) HASTY SYNTHETIC-TO-REAL GENERALIZATION — 'works on synthetic ⇒ works in the world.' This is endemic: every corruption benchmark (Robo3D, Dong) explicitly flags sim≠all-real; CARLA closed-loop (Schreier) flags sim-to-real gap. Antidote = state synthetic-only as a scope limit, validate direction on a real substrate, and treat the synthetic→real swap as a place where NEW failure modes appear (occquery's own real-data audit is the proof: synthetic F1=1.00, real precision 0/5).
(3) CIRCULAR ORACLE — proving correctness against a GT that is the model's own output or shares its data source (Occ3D's 3D-to-2D consistency; any self-agreement). Antidote = an independent reference (simulation with true geometry; a different sensor; SOS soundness).
(4) CORRELATION-AS-CAUSATION / WRONG PROXY — 'nearest agent matters' (proximity), 'high clean accuracy ⇒ robust', 'vertical image position ⇒ distance.' Antidotes = the Waymo influence-over-proximity KL (causal-flavored, validated by removal); Robo3D/Dong decoupling robustness from clean accuracy; Why-Far-Looks-Up DERIVING the perspective-projection law (v(Z)∝1/Z) then predicting the counter-perspective collapse — deduction licenses the causal claim before the test.

THE RULE: declare your camp; if inductive, add a first-principles scaffold and a counterfactual that removes the claimed cause; if deductive, the proof IS the result but you must enumerate the scope/assumptions as the only failure surface; refuse all four fallacies by construction, not by disclaimer.

**Maps to spatial-probe:** STRONG / KEEP, with one named live risk. spatial-probe is explicitly a HYBRID-of-camps program: H1 expressivity is a quasi-DEDUCTIVE construction (a non-identifiability proof: identical box+map inputs MUST yield identical answers — docs/expressivity-vs-refav.md:50-61), while H3 denotation is INDUCTIVE/empirical. It refuses all four fallacies in writing: (1) affirming-the-consequent is pre-empted by the kill condition 'if box-only matches our denotation quality the angle fails' (PLAN.md:192) — the claim is structured as a remove-it test against the box baseline. (2) hasty synthetic-to-real is the headline house rule ('Synthetic results are not scientific results', PLAN.md:202-204) and was VINDICATED by the real-data audit where synthetic F1=1.00 collapsed to real precision 0/5 (h3-real-data-findings.md:60-69) — the single best in-repo demonstration of trap #2 in the whole corpus. (3) circular-oracle is named and a leg was DROPPED for it (anchors:216-225; and H3's current pass is self-flagged as circular 'visual-agreement', not evidence, h3:87-89). (4) correlation-as-causation / wrong-proxy is the deepest insight in the repo: the '0/5 precision' was re-read as a CATEGORY ERROR — a static geometric measurement graded against a dynamic-danger answer key; scene-0061's 0.8m is a CORRECT measurement of the gap beside a LEAD CAR, and 'that's a lead car not a wall' is a dynfield (causal/temporal) verdict, not an occquery failure (PLAN.md:124-137; h3:189-214). That is precisely the Waymo proximity-vs-influence distinction applied to your own instrument. LIVE RISK / FIX: the program leans almost entirely INDUCTIVE for H3 and stages 2-6; per the corpus, add the first-principles scaffold where cheap — e.g. occquery already HAS a deductive core in H1, but H3's denotation claim would be strengthened by a Why-Far-Looks-Up-style geometric derivation of WHEN voxel-resolution makes a clearance unrecoverable (the 0.4m sub-voxel bound is noted, h3:210-214, but not formalized into a derived error model that PREDICTS the failure before measuring it).

## 7. Scope discipline + threats to validity (separate perception error from claim error; control the confound up front)

FRONTIER PATTERN. The corpus's signature threat-to-validity control is SEPARATING THE STAGE WHERE ERROR ENTERS so a claim is attributed to the right cause. The canonical moves: (a) report results under MULTIPLE track/box sources to split perception error from grounding error (RefAV: GT tracks vs LE3DE2E vs LT3D; nuScenes-QA: GT boxes 84.3% vs detected −0.6%, quantifying that the gap is mostly REASONING not perception); (b) hold the downstream stack FIXED so only the studied variable moves (Schreier drops 16 detectors into one CARLA stack; PKL conditions on GT trajectories); (c) build a CONTROLLED oracle where exactly ONE factor varies (SpatialTunnel decorrelates vertical-position from depth; ProcFront decouples layout from instance; the indoor-domain-gap paper HOLDS TAXONOMY FIXED and excludes rare/structural objects to isolate the true visual-distribution shift, reporting a directional A→B matrix instead of a symmetric average that would hide asymmetry); (d) NAME AND NEUTRALIZE THE SHORTCUT/CONFOUND UP FRONT, adversarially — treat the shortcut as the DEFAULT hypothesis to rule out, not an afterthought (train a vision-blind text-only solver on the test set and prune what it can solve; report frequency baselines so 'most-frequent-answer' performance is visible).

SCOPE DISCIPLINE specifically: the strongest papers FENCE the instrument to what it measures and refuse adjacent claims. SparseOcc's cascading-error kill is a scope fence (the mask transformer is confined to whatever geometry the sparse decoder kept). The indoor-domain-gap paper explicitly positions itself as 'benchmarks + datasets, NOT a solution.' Several occupancy papers FAIL this — they smuggle danger/semantics into a geometric metric, or average over horizons to hide decay.

GENERALIZATION-AXIS PARTITIONING is the standard external-validity control: pre-split evaluation into in-distribution vs novel-object/background/environment (RT-2, OpenVLA), or held-out-from-training human benchmarks (SpatialVLM, SpatialRGPT), so 'it generalizes' is tested on data the model provably never saw; cross-embodiment/cross-platform demonstration substitutes for a held-out split where a fixed test set is impractical (OpenVLA multi-robot; ConceptGraphs Jackal + Spot).

THE RULE: attribute every error to its stage by varying one input at a time (track source, downstream stack, single confound); name the most likely shortcut and rule it out adversarially before claiming the capability; FENCE the instrument — refuse claims that need a different instrument (a different time-scale, a different sensor); partition seen-vs-unseen so generalization is tested out-of-distribution.

**Maps to spatial-probe:** BEST-IN-CLASS / KEEP — this is spatial-probe's strongest single discipline and it exceeds most surveyed work. (1) Stage-separation is the entire H1/H2/H3 architecture: H3 runs predicates over a RELEASED third-party occupancy field (not one you produce) so denotation error is attributed to the predicate, not to your occupancy estimator (PLAN.md:156-164) — exactly the RefAV multi-track-source / nuScenes-QA GT-vs-detected split. ## 8. Limitations + conclusion form (strong-claim-then-fence; instrument-vs-diagnosis; pick your lane)

FRONTIER PATTERN. Conclusion form is remarkably uniform and you should match the rigorous end of it. The embodied/VLA survey's dominant form is STRONG-CLAIM-THEN-FENCE: restate the hypothesis as confirmed, summarize what was ablated to earn it, then explicitly NAME the boundary the method cannot cross (RT-2: 'does not acquire any ability to perform new motions — limited to skills in robot data'; OpenVLA: single-image input, control frequency, sub-90% reliability; SpatialRGPT: AABB label-geometry caps accuracy; ConceptGraphs: LVLM-caption errors propagate into planning). The most RIGOROUS limitation names a STRUCTURAL boundary (a class the method cannot cross) or an UPSTREAM ORACLE/LABEL CEILING that caps achievable accuracy — not a vague 'room to improve.' Register is intensifier-light and evidence-anchored; the one outlier ('consistently and definitively outperform', Diffusion Policy) still ties the adjective to a 12-task sweep.

TWO CONCLUSION ARCHETYPES — and the corpus says PICK ONE per paper: (i) INSTRUMENT/ENGINEERING — ship a reusable module/score, prove value via a downstream remove-it gain (GLENet, ActiveAD, Waymo interactivity, the mapping systems Voxblox/FIESTA/nvblox/Voxfield conclude 'we built X that beats Y', revisable by the next faster system); (ii) DIAGNOSIS/BENCHMARK — ship infrastructure + a measured deficit, prove necessity via the SIZE of the gap to humans/oracle (CAPTURe ~7-14× worse than humans; VSI-Bench 33.8pt; the indoor-domain-gap paper 'NOT a solution'). The deductive camp has a third: EXISTENCE-OF-GUARANTEE ('we proved property P is achievable', permanent given assumptions — IRIS/C-IRIS/HJ). Forecasting/world-model papers add a PRESCRIPTIVE note (recommended supervision: 3D flow, inflated GMO, flow-trace consistency). The corpus-wide weakness to avoid: 'contribution-announcement + community-aspiration' ('first benchmark', a SOTA number, 'we hope this boosts the field') is NOT hypothesis-resolution — the rigorous conclusion resolves the stated hypothesis (confirmed/falsified/scoped), it does not merely announce.

THE RULE: state limitations as a dedicated plain-spoken section naming a STRUCTURAL boundary or an oracle ceiling (not a softener); conclude in ONE lane (instrument-proven-by-remove-it OR diagnosis-proven-by-gap-size OR guarantee-proven-by-construction); resolve the hypothesis explicitly; for any threshold-bearing claim, the conclusion must restate the relative load-bearing gap, not the movable absolute; keep adjectives tied to the sweep.

**Maps to spatial-probe:** STRONG / KEEP, with one lane-choice to make explicit. spatial-probe already writes limitations as STRUCTURAL boundaries, not softeners — the corpus's gold standard: the voxel-resolution ceiling ('at 0.4m a 0.07m sub-voxel gap cannot be told from contact; finer clearance needs raw-LiDAR re-voxelization, not a predicate change', PLAN.md:135-137; h3:210-214) is an upstream-data ceiling exactly like SpatialRGPT's AABB ceiling; the H3-is-circular admission is an oracle-ceiling fence (h3:6-10); the danger-is-dynfield boundary is a structural class-the-instrument-cannot-cross fence (PLAN.md:124-137). The LANE is mostly chosen correctly and consciously: occquery's headline lane is DIAGNOSIS/EXISTENCE via H1 (an oracle-free expressivity gap, proven by the size of the inexpressibility, like the benchmark papers) — and the docs even tell you to 'lead with the part that needs no oracle' (anchors:25). The RIDE/DEFINE/HYBRID taxonomy (anchors:12-16) is precisely the 'pick your conclusion form per topic' rule operationalized: RIDE→beat-SOTA (instrument/engineering lane), DEFINE→public-data + measurable-gap + released-benchmark (diagnosis lane).
